Common Questions

Things people ask
before we start.

What kind of brands do you typically work with?
D2C founders, small-to-mid businesses in the UAE and Kerala, creators building personal brands, and operators who want to ship without an agency overhead. Some recent work: automotive (Motorwagon, Motorium, Metrocars), fashion (Envogue), lifestyle (Nardas, Zuhoori), and D2C wellness (VellyEyes).
Do you work with brands outside India and the UAE?
Yes. VellyEyes is a US-targeted brand. Currently building toward serving GCC, South Asia, and US markets. The advantage of working with an operator is the work moves with you, not the postal code.
What's your typical engagement length?
Project-based engagements run 4–12 weeks depending on scope (a single funnel build versus a full growth system). Retainer engagements are quarterly minimums so we have enough runway to actually see results compound.
How quickly can you start?
A 30-minute discovery call within 48 hours of you reaching out. Strategy work typically starts within the same week. First deliverables (ads, video, landing page) go live in days, not months — that's the operator model.
What if I'm a small business with a tight budget?
Honest answer: budget matters more than agency size. I work with small budgets all the time — what doesn't work is no budget. If you have under ₹50k/month ad budget plus retainer, I'll point you toward the digital skills training instead, where you learn to run things yourself.
What's the difference between your brands and client work?
I'm primarily a D2C operator — I run paid ads (Meta + TikTok), build Shopify funnels, and ship AI UGC creatives. I take on D2C client work and 1-to-1 mentoring under the withfaijas brand. All work is remote, operated from Kerala, deployed worldwide.
